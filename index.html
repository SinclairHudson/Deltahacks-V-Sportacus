What is Sportacus?

Sportacus is a platform that allows community members to connect with each other in order to exercise and play sports.

How does it Work?

Create a Profile

Create a personalized profile with minutes. Add all your sports, activities and interests, and a way to contact you. Set a username and profile picture, and you’re done!

Find a Partner

Sportacus looks at looks at your interests, and finds people with activities in common. Browse this list and reach out to find someone to exercise with!

Enjoy!

That’s it! Collect points as you interact with users in the platform, and meet people who share your interests.
